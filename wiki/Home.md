# digger wiki

**Quick reference**. For the full docs site, see
[pq-cybarg.github.io/digger](https://pq-cybarg.github.io/digger/).

`digger` is a cross-platform endpoint forensics suite. Runs entirely on
your machine. Codified ethics. Post-quantum signed evidence. 28
detectors including a defensive mirror of every offensive-tooling
kill-chain phase.
